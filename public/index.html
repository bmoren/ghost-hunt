WELCOME TO
GHOST HUNT
In this game, you will randomly assigned to the realm of the living, or the life of the dead. Depending on your fate, it will be imperative that you succesfully HUNT or HIDE.
Don't forget, you are not alone in your endeavors.
HUNTERS must work together to track and expel the ghost. Shine your light on it, and that should be enough to dispel the spirit. Don’t forget, even if you can’t see them, the GHOSTS can see you.
GHOSTS must work together to avoid the HUNTERS. Be wary spirits, you may not be visible to the HUNTERS, but by passing through objects, you alert them to your position.
Good luck, and Happy Haunting!
